First, make a layer of Parle OR Marie biscuits dipped in milk.
Then, use chocolate cake crumbs  for the second layer.
Put a layer of chocolate OR vanilla ice-cream.
Repeat the process again. (Put a second layer of biscuits, chocolate cake crumbs and ice-cream)
Put some cake crumbs (optional) and some Hersheys chocolate syrup on top.
Put the pudding in the refrigerator and allow it to cool for about 2 hours.
The chocolate pudding is ready! Enjoy!
